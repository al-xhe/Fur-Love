Our Adoption Process

Every year, we find new, caring homes for thousands of homeless pets. Each pet is unique, so when you apply to adopt, our team ensures you can offer everything they need to thrive.

Adding a new pet to your family is a big decision, but we're here to make it as easy as possible. Learn how you can prepare to adopt and set both you and your new pet up for success.

How to Adopt a Pet from FurLove:



  1. Start Your Search:
    Browse our pets currently looking for a new home.

  2. Express Your Interest:
    Fill out our online form.

  3. Application Received:
    We’ll confirm we’ve received your application.

  4. Contact Within 10 Days:
    We’ll get in touch with you.

  5. Meet the Pet:
    You’ll be invited to meet the pet.

  6. Taking Your Pet Home:
    Complete the adoption and bring your new friend home.

Adoption Requirements

To adopt a pet, you must:

    - Be at least 18 years old
    - Provide a government-issued photo ID
    - Complete a preliminary questionnaire and meet with an Adoption Counselor

Although meet-and-greets with resident pets are not required, we encourage you to introduce your pet to your potential adoptee to ensure a good fit for your family. Our team members are here to assist you.
