Volunteer

Volunteers are at the heart of everything we do, and we'd love you to join us.

Whatever time you can give and whatever your background, if you're for pets, we're for you.

Our volunteering roles

Adoption Counselor

These volunteers guide potential adopters through the process, matching them with the right pet and providing information about pet care and adoption requirements.

Animal Care Assistant

Volunteers help with feeding, grooming, and exercising the pets, ensuring they stay healthy and happy while waiting for their forever homes.

Social Media Coordinator

By managing the center's online presence, volunteers promote available pets, share success stories, and engage with the community to boost adoptions.

Event Organizer

Volunteers plan and coordinate adoption events, fundraisers, and awareness campaigns, helping to raise the center's profile and increase pet adoptions.

Kennel Cleaner

These volunteers maintain clean and sanitary living spaces for the pets, contributing to their well-being and creating a welcoming atmosphere for visitors.

Foster Care Provider

Volunteers offer temporary homes to pets in need, giving them a safe and loving environment until they find a permanent family.

Success Stories

Real-life stories of unconditional love, companionship, and limitless snuggles

From Sheltered to Spoiled: Bunnies Journey

Hopper, a gentle gray bunny, was rescued and adopted by the Evans family. With a comfy hutch and plenty of fresh greens, Hopper quickly became part of the family. Now, he spends his days hopping around and snuggling with his new owners, bringing joy and laughter to their home..

Luna’s Leap of Faith: From Fearful to Fearless

Luna, a timid Terrier mix, was rescued and taken to the shelter. The Taylor family adopted her, providing the gentle care she needed. With their love and patience, Luna’s confidence grew. Now, she’s a happy, playful pup, thriving and adored in her new home.

Buddy’s Big Leap: From Street to Sweetheart

Buddy, a stray Beagle, was found on the streets and brought to the shelter. The Garcia family, drawn to his playful spirit, adopted him. Buddy quickly adjusted, making friends and becoming a family favorite. His energy and affection have made him a cherished member of the Garcia family, proving that a second chance brings lasting joy.

Bella’s Second Chance: A New Lease on Life

Here’s a shorter version: Bella, a senior tabby, was brought to the shelter after her owner passed away. The Johnsons, seeking a mature cat, adopted Bella and welcomed her warmly. She quickly adapted, becoming a loving lap cat and a cherished companion. Bella's gentle nature has brought comfort and joy to the Johnsons, showing that age is no barrier to finding a loving home.
